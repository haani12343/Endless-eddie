Endless Eddie is an endless runer game with a retro music touch to it which makes the game more attractive and fun to play
collect coins and create records and try to beat your highest records to make new ones
this game gives a really calm vibe which helps to reduce stress of the person who is playing the game
the more you survive the higher the speed of the player 
if you hit an obstacle you restart at the starting point of the game wiht zero coins and score
a very simple game yet the best to get rid of your ovethinking and stress
keep jumping and dodging the obstacles to survive
# how to play:
hit space to jump
